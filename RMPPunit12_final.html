Reflective Essay:

The Research Methods and Professional Practice module that I under took at the University of Essex Online served to be one of the modules I did not enjoy. Being a practical person, theory modules have always been a weakness in my studies when the theory does not a line towards an interest I might share in fully. The module did highlight the importance however of conducting proper research. Research is an aspect many overlook and don’t fully take into consideration in any aspect of life. We could be researching a new phone for ourselves or for a new place to stay. Ensuring that our research is done properly will assist in aiding everyday life in being that little bit easier.

In terms to our assessments and modules, the research aspect did become tedious at times resulting in assignments taking longer than they should and a lot of rabbit holes being dug while research was being done. The topic of choice did prove to be interesting, however, a lack of free journals and articles did prohibit a lot of in-depth research from taking place. However, a literature review was still done and research proposal produced for the module. 

What did I learn from this module?

The importance of research. We have taken research for granted and do not realise the amount of effort it actually takes to produce research journals and articles. Understanding how journals and articles need to be structured makes reading and understanding said articles a lot easier. Understanding research proposals as well provides a new feeling of gratitude towards those who constantly conduct research for everyone working in the industry. 

Research Methods and Professional Practice lacks practical aspects and is solely based on theory. This can hinder those who do not enjoy theory to have a tough time in completing the module. A lot of calculations that can be done in the module can be done through programs such as Microsoft Excel or similar applications. Therefore, this module as a whole can be considered a theoretical module. 

What was my experience on working alone for the module?

Considering that most of the modules I have worked on I have had exceptional team mates to work with, I did find completing this module to be a bit more difficult with a lack of team mates to talk to and bounce ideas off of. In modules where I am unfamiliar with the process, it helps to bounce off ideas with other team members to gain a sense of direction and understanding to what is expected to complete the module. This is not the same for modules where the knowledge is already existent. 

However, due to previous experiences, it has also been a pleasure to work alone without certain teammates holding the process back from being completed. At times, teammates would have been of great assistance, but overall, the experience was very mixed with a new found respect for team work as well in areas where you may not be strong. 

The impact the module had in Professional Development:

This module has shed new light on the significance on research in cyber security. In a world where IT is evolving constantly, keeping up to date is critical to avoid being left behind and being outdated. By continuously researching latest technological trends, developments and news articles, you can stay up to date the way that you need to ensuring that no new threat goes undetected.

The impact the module had in Personal Development:

Having the foundation to conduct proper research will aid any aspect of life where research needs to be done. By taking the skills learnt in this module and applying it to your everyday life, your research skills will improve the knowledge you have on any topic you might need to learn about. It is critical not to underestimate the impact tertiary studies can have on our personal development and assist us in our everyday lives.

Conclusion
 Overall, the Research Methods and Professional Practice module taught individuals the importance of conducting research correctly and creating proper research proposals. These skills will greatly assist any individual in a professional or personal capacity. We are in a world that is constantly changing. Ensuring that we keep up with these changes is critical to success for any individual or business and this is achievable through the use of proper research proposals and literature reviews.
